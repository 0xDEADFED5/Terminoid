What is this?

This website hosts and plays recordings that adhere to this format: asciicast v2

It's an extremely simple version of: asciinema-server

Why?

Because I wanted a player that supports all the control codes that xterm.js does.

Privacy?

This website doesn't require or want any of your personal information. Also, it's free and open source.

Limitations?

There is no search and there are no accounts.

Upload limits = 16MiB max (after being gzipped by browser) and 1 upload per minute per IP. I can increase if needed.

Features?

You can upload or play recordings and optionally delete them from your personal link after uploading. That's about it so far.

License?

BSD-3-Clause

What's Terminoid built with?

Backend: OpenResty + Redis

Front: xterm.js, pako, a fork of html2canvas, terminal.css, FiraCode font
